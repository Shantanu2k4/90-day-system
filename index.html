Three-phase roadmap

Phase 1 — Days 1–30: Install the system

Hit all 5 sessions every week. Eat on plan 6/7 days. Target: 98 kg. Your Phase 1 habit poster is already in place — this plan is Phase 1 in action. Do not miss a single Monday. The first four weeks build the neural pathways that make everything after automatic.

Phase 2 — Days 31–60: Add progressive overload

Add weight to every lift weekly. Zero cheat meals this phase. Target: 94 kg. Around week 5 you will feel a mental clarity shift — your brain running on a clean deficit and good sleep is noticeably different.

Phase 3 — Days 61–90: Peak execution

Replace 1 roti/day with extra sabzi. Increase calisthenics to 3x/week. Target: 90 kg. By Day 90 you will look like a different person in the mirror. Take the same photo from Day 0. That comparison is your reward.

The only rule that matters

You do not feel like going. You go anyway.

Every excuse you have made before — tired after shift, too stressed, need to study — will appear again. This system is designed so that even on your worst day, you have a clear path. The question is not whether you can do this. It is whether you respect yourself enough to stop stalling.

Progressive overload — the only rule for muscle growth

Every week, either add 2.5 kg to at least one compound lift OR add 1–2 reps to two exercises. If you are not tracking weights, you are not progressing. Take a photo of your log after every session — that is your proof of work.

Non-negotiable diet rules

  1. Soya chunks in at least one meal every single day — no exceptions, no substitutions.
  2. No biscuits, chips, namkeen, or fried snacks during shift. Roasted chana is your only snack.
  3. 4 litres of water per day. First thing after waking: 500 ml before anything else.
  4. Maximum 4 rotis per day total. Additional hunger = drink water, wait 10 minutes.
  5. One bad meal does not make a bad day. Reset at the very next meal — do not let one slip cascade.
  6. No chai with sugar on shift. Black coffee or plain water only after 22:30 (caffeine half-life is 6 hours).

iPhone 12 rules — effective today

  1. Grayscale on. Settings → Accessibility → Display & Text Size → Color Filters → Greyscale. Colourful apps hijack attention by design. Remove the trigger.
  2. Study window locked. 13:30–16:00: airplane mode. No exceptions. Studying with notifications on is not studying — it is performance.
  3. Screen Time limits. Instagram + YouTube combined: 45 minutes/day, unlockable only after 17:30. Settings → Screen Time → App Limits.
  4. Phone-free first 20 minutes. After waking, no phone for 20 minutes. Drink water, eat, stretch first. Your day starts with you — not with someone else's content.
  5. Phone charges on your desk. Not beside your bed. This one change alone improves sleep quality within a week.
  6. The fine. If you scroll outside allowed hours for more than 15 minutes: 20 push-ups before you continue. Consequence changes behaviour — motivation does not.

Pre-sleep protocol — 04:00 to 04:45

What you do in this 45-minute window determines how you feel at 13:00 and whether your afternoon study session is focused or wasted.

  1. Eat your pre-sleep meal (oats + peanut butter) immediately on reaching home. Nutrition in before the system winds down.
  2. Change out of work clothes. This is not optional — it signals to your body that the shift is over.
  3. Dim all lights. Switch to warm light only.
  4. No phone. Maximum 5 minutes of messages if genuinely urgent. Nothing else.
  5. Quick 5-minute stretch: neck rolls, shoulder circles, lower back. Night shift wrecks posture — this prevents cumulative injury.
  6. Eye mask is mandatory. Buy one this week (₹100–150). Mumbai daylight at 6 am will cut your sleep by 2 hours without it.
  7. Fan or AC on. Cooler room = deeper, faster sleep — this is physiologically proven.

Morning routine — 13:00 to 13:35

Do not open social media until this sequence is complete. Every single day.

  1. Drink the 500 ml water you left on your desk before sleeping.
  2. 5 minutes near a window or outside in sunlight — resets your circadian clock even on an inverted schedule.
  3. Read The Daily Stoic entry. Write your 3 sentences in GoodNotes.
  4. First meal: sattu drink.
  5. On Tue / Thu / Sat: BetterMe calisthenics now (13:15–13:35), then shower, then study.

Sleep rules

  • Never sleep past 13:30, including Sundays. Cycle consistency matters more than extra hours.
  • If you cannot sleep by 05:30: lie still or read something boring. Do not pick up your phone — light resets melatonin and makes the next night worse.
  • No caffeine after 22:30. Tea, coffee, and even cola — all have a 5–6 hour half-life.
  • If you feel groggy at 13:00: cold water on your face, then step outside for 5 minutes. Do not lie back down under any circumstances.

The stall protocol

If weight stops dropping for 2 consecutive Sundays:

  1. Do not change the training. First variable to fix is always food.
  2. Cut 100 kcal: remove 1 roti per day. That is it. Small, precise adjustment.
  3. Track water — dehydration causes false scale readings.
  4. If stall continues a third week: add 10 minutes of walking on 2 rest days.
